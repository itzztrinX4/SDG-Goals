Sustainable Development Goals (SDG)


The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are also known as Global Goals. They were adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015 to set a goal to end poverty, protect the planet and ensure that all people enjoy prosperity and peace by 2030.
